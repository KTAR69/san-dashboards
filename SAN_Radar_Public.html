BOJ at 1.0%, highest since 1995. Watch the trend, not any single month.

Sources: Katayama remarks · Fortune · CNBC / TIC data
Quarterly TIC data · GPIF allocation moves within the ±6% bands · BOJ's next decision.
The canary in the coal mine isn't the selling — it's WHO ISN'T REPLACING the buyer. When the largest foreign holder eases out of the reserve asset, watch which hands ease in: draft U.S. rules push stablecoin reserves INTO Treasuries just as Japan drifts out. The old holders leave by the front door while the private ramp gets marched in the back. Note: a viral claim of a "$6 trillion Monday dump" is ~6x Japan's entire Treasury holding — the current is real; the panic arithmetic is not.
